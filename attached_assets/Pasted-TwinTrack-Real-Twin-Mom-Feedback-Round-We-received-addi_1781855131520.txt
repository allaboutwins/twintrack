TwinTrack — Real Twin Mom Feedback Round 💕🚀

We received additional feedback from active twin moms. Please prioritize the following fixes and improvements.

---

1. NAP LOGGING ISSUES (HIGH PRIORITY)

Feedback:

* Manual nap entries sometimes do not register when tapping buttons.
* Play/start nap button sometimes does not respond.
* Manual nap duration options feel too limited.

Please investigate:

* button click reliability
* mobile touch handling
* state update issues
* nap timer start logic

Also add quick nap buttons:

* 15 min
* 20 min
* 30 min
* 45 min
* 1 hour
* 1.5 hours
* 2 hours

Goal:
Faster logging for exhausted parents.

---

2. NIGHT SLEEP CALCULATION BUG 🌙

Feedback:

Night sleep totals appear incorrect.

Example:
A user reports only ~19 total hours showing for an entire week despite sleeping 7–10 hours per night.

Please audit:

* cross-midnight sleep calculations
* overnight sleep sessions
* weekly aggregation logic
* stats calculations
* timezone interactions

This is a high-priority trust issue.

---

3. FEEDBACK BUTTON POSITION

Feedback:

"The feedback button is in the way."

Please review:

* placement
* overlap with content
* mobile responsiveness

Goal:
Keep feedback accessible without blocking important actions.

---

4. STATS → DAILY DETAIL VIEW

Feedback:

Parents need to see exact event times when reviewing past days.

Especially important for:

* medications
* supplements
* iron doses
* feeding schedules

Please improve daily stats/history pages to show:

* timestamp
* entry type
* notes
* medication name if logged

Goal:
Parents can confidently verify what happened on a specific day.

---

5. DIAPER NOTES 💩

Feedback:

Users want notes on diaper entries.

Please add optional diaper notes:

Examples:

* color
* consistency
* small amount
* unusual observations

Similar to bottle/feed notes.

---

6. COPY TO OTHER TWIN 👶👶

Feedback:

Parents frequently log the same activity for both twins.

Please add:

"Copy to Twin B"
"Copy to Twin A"

Examples:

* medication
* supplements
* vitamins
* routines
* sleep
* feeds

Goal:
Reduce duplicate entry effort.

---

7. MANUAL NAP ENTRY FLEXIBILITY

Please allow:

* custom hours
* custom minutes

Not only preset durations.
