Next Steps for TwinTrack 🚀

The app foundation is already amazing. Now let’s evolve TwinTrack from a tracking app into a full “Twin Parenting Ecosystem” that keeps moms engaged daily and emotionally connected to the All About Twins brand.

PRIORITY IMPROVEMENTS:

1) Replace “Twins TV” Branding
The current “Twins TV” tab feels slightly disconnected from the utility/tracking experience.

Please test new names such as:
- Learn
- Expert Videos
- Twin Tips
- Academy
- Twin Hub

Recommended choice:
👉 “Learn”

It feels clean, premium, educational, and emotionally calming.

The section should become:
“A supportive twin parenting learning library.”

6) Add Community Feeling Without Building Full Social Media

Simple ideas:

- “Twin Mom Quote”
- “You’re doing amazing”
- Polls later
- Community milestones later

The app should emotionally support exhausted parents.

--------------------------------------------------

7) Push Notifications (Important)

YES — build notifications next.

But the tone must feel supportive and calm, never stressful or clinical.

Good examples:
- “Twin A may be ready for a feeding soon 💕”
- “Time for a little diaper check 👶”
- “You’re doing amazing today ✨”
- “Twin B’s nap may be ending soon 😴”

Avoid robotic medical wording.
