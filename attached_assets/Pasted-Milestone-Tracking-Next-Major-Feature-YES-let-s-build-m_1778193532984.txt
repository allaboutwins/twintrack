Milestone Tracking — Next Major Feature 💕

YES — let’s build milestone tracking next.

But the goal should NOT be:
“store baby milestones.”

The goal should be:
“help twin parents emotionally remember their journey.”

This feature should feel warm, visual, emotional, and celebratory.

--------------------------------------------------

CORE EXPERIENCE

Each twin should have:
- Their own milestone timeline
- Photo uploads
- Notes/memories
- Dates achieved
- Optional comparison view between twins

Suggested milestone categories:
- First Smile
- First Laugh
- Rolled Over
- Sat Up
- Crawled
- First Tooth
- First Word
- First Steps
- Slept Through the Night
- First Daycare Day
- First Birthday
- Potty Training
- First Day of School

Also allow:
👉 “Custom Milestone”

Twin parents LOVE documenting unique moments.

--------------------------------------------------

IMPORTANT UX IDEA:
Celebrate milestones beautifully.

When a parent logs one:
- soft animation/confetti
- encouraging message
- emotional tone

Examples:
“Twin A just unlocked a beautiful new milestone 💕”
“You’ll never forget this moment ✨”

This creates emotional attachment to the app.

--------------------------------------------------

PHOTO MEMORIES

Each milestone should support:
- photo upload
- caption/note
- optional short memory

Example:
“Twin B took her first steps toward her sister 🥹”

This turns the app into a memory keepsake, not just a tracker.

--------------------------------------------------

SMART TWIN COMPARISON (VERY IMPORTANT)

Twin parents naturally compare development.

Build this carefully and gently.

GOOD:
“Twin A reached this milestone 2 weeks earlier 💕 Every twin develops differently.”

BAD:
Anything competitive or alarming.

Tone matters enormously.

--------------------------------------------------

FUTURE EXPANSION IDEAS

Later this system could support:
- yearly memory recap
- printable memory book
- growth timeline
- milestone slideshow
- birthday recap videos
- AI-generated “Twin Journey” summaries

This could become one of the most emotionally valuable parts of TwinTrack.

--------------------------------------------------

VERY IMPORTANT:
Do NOT make this feature feel medical.

Avoid:
- clinical charts
- scary comparisons
- pressure-driven tracking

The emotional feeling should be:
“capturing precious twin memories.”

--------------------------------------------------

LONG-TERM VISION

TwinTrack is evolving into:
part tracker + part memory book + part support system + part education hub.

That positioning is MUCH stronger than a normal baby tracking app.
