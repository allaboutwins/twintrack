TwinTrack — Premium Positioning + Retention Improvements 💕🚀

A few additional improvements based on product strategy and user psychology.

---

1. PUMPING BUTTON

Please replace the current pumping emoji:

🫙

with:

🥛 Pumping

Reason:

* clearer meaning
* better visibility on desktop
* less confusion than breastfeeding or bottle-feeding icons

Please use:

🥛 Pumping

consistently across the app.

---

2. MONTHLY ACTIVE USER EMAILS (HIGH PRIORITY)

Please begin planning a monthly email for ACTIVE TwinTrack users.

Goal:

* increase engagement
* increase retention
* encourage feature discovery
* strengthen emotional connection

IMPORTANT:

This should NOT feel like a sales email.

Tone:

* warm
* supportive
* helpful

Example structure:

💕 Your TwinTrack Monthly Recap

This month:

* feeds logged
* sleep sessions tracked
* diapers logged
* memories captured
* milestones celebrated

Then introduce feature discovery naturally:

Many TwinTrack families also used:

✓ Twin AI - Write a common question asked by twin parents

✓ Caregiver Access - Send an invitation to your partner / babysitter etc.

✓ Twins Academy - Learn the best tips for a better twins sleep  

✓ Full Magazine Library - Did you read the latest Twins Magazine 

✓ Premium Memory Cards 

✓ New community features - Latest polls answered / questions Asked 

The goal is:

👉 "I didn't know TwinTrack could do that."

NOT:

👉 "Buy now."

4. PREMIUM PAGE POSITIONING

Please lead with the monthly equivalent pricing.

Reason:

Twin moms naturally compare:

* monthly costs
* subscriptions
* coffee purchases

More easily than annual totals.

Example messaging:

💕 Founding Moms

Only $3.25/month

Less than a coffee each month ☕

Keep:

(Billed annually at $39/year)

as smaller supporting text underneath.
