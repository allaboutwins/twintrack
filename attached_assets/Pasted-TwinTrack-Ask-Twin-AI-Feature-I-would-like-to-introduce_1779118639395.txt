TwinTrack — ✨ Ask Twin AI Feature 💕

I would like to introduce a new feature inside TwinTrack:

👉 “Ask Twin AI”

Goal:
Create an emotional, supportive AI assistant specially designed for:

- twin moms
- twin parents
- NICU families
- triplets/quads families
- exhausted caregivers needing quick support

This should NOT feel like a generic chatbot.

It should feel like: a calm, supportive twin-parenting companion.

---

CORE IDEA

Twin AI should learn from:

- TwinTrack app interactions
- tracking patterns
- repeated parent questions
- community insights
- twin parenting content
- Twins Academy content later
- FAQs
- common twin parenting struggles

Goal:
Over time, Twin AI becomes smarter specifically for multiple families.

Examples of questions:

- “How do I sync twin naps?”
- “My twins wake each other up — what should I do?”
- “How much formula should my 2 month old twins drink?”
- “Ideas for tandem feeding?”
- “How do adjusted ages work for preemies?”
- “Twin bedtime routine ideas?”
- “How do I survive twin sleep deprivation?”

---

IMPORTANT SAFETY DISCLAIMER 🔔

This is VERY important.

Twin AI responses should NEVER appear as medical advice.

Please add a visible disclaimer near:

- the input area
- AI answers
- onboarding/use

Suggested wording:

🔔 Disclaimer: Twin AI responses are provided for peer support and educational purposes only and are not medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider or pediatrician for professional medical guidance.

Please make this:

- visible
- warm
- non-scary
- trust-building

---

DESIGN DIRECTION

The experience should feel:

- soft
- emotionally supportive
- calming
- modern
- trustworthy
- not robotic

Please avoid:

- cold AI styling
- overly technical appearance

Use:

- gentle cards
- reassuring empty states
- conversational UX
- emotionally supportive wording

Phase 1 ideas:
