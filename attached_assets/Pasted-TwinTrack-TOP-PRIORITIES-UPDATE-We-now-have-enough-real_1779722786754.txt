TwinTrack — TOP PRIORITIES UPDATE 🚀  
We now have enough real-world usage data to clearly understand where retention/value comes from.

Please pause non-essential/new experimental features for now and focus development on the CORE DAILY RETENTION LOOP:

LOG → SEE INSIGHT → RETURN

The goal is to make TwinTrack feel essential every single day for twin parents.

Here are the highest priorities in order:

---

1. HISTORICAL STATS / INSIGHTS 📈 (HIGHEST PRIORITY)

Right now moms log data, but they don’t feel the value of logging long-term.

We need beautiful, emotional, easy-to-read historical insights.

Please build:

Daily / Weekly / Monthly views for:
- Sleep
- Feeding
- Diapers
- Pumping (future-ready)
- Medication (future-ready)

Include:
- averages
- totals
- trends
- comparisons
- “best streak”
- “average sleep”
- “feeds per day”
- “last poop”
- “days logged”

Important:
- mobile-first
- calming design
- visual/simple
- not overly clinical

This is CRITICAL for retention.

Goal:
make moms feel:
👉 “I’m building useful data every day.”

Future premium:
- doctor PDF exports
- printable reports
- advanced analytics
- predictions

---

2. FEEDING IMPROVEMENTS 🍼 (VERY IMPORTANT)

Feeding is currently the strongest usage signal in the app.

Please improve feeding tracking significantly.

Add:
- oz/ml tracking
- breastfeeding duration
- left/right breast
- pumping tracking
- solids details
- medication tracking support
- editable feed times
- editable amounts after logging

Also:
create better feeding summaries:
- total intake today
- per twin
- averages
- last feed
- time since feed

This is especially important for:
- NICU/preemie twins
- adjusted age tracking
- weight gain monitoring

This may become our strongest premium feature later.

---

3. NOTIFICATIONS / REMINDERS 🔔

TwinTrack needs to become a “second brain” for overwhelmed twin parents.

Please begin implementing lightweight smart reminders:
- feeding reminders
- sleep reminders
- medication reminders (future-ready)
- pumping reminders (future-ready)

Examples:
- “It’s been 3 hours since Twin A’s last feed”
- “Twin B may be ready for a nap soon”
- “You haven’t logged today yet”

Also:
create a notification history center using the 🍒 cherries icon.

This is a major retention feature.

---

- smoother transitions

Right now logging feels like work.

- “Great job keeping feeds synced today”
- “Twin A slept 42 mins longer today”
- “You logged 5 days in a row”
- “Your twins had their best sleep streak this week”

Goal:
create emotional encouragement + habit loops.

The app should feel:
- warm
- supportive
- calming
- rewarding

NOT:
cold or clinical.
